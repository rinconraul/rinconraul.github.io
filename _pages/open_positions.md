#### The group is growing

The Rincon Research Group is being built at CU Boulder, and this is where future
PhD students, postdocs, and collaborators will appear. If you are drawn to
problems at the intersection of **data, uncertainty, computation, and societal
impact** in engineered systems, I would like to hear from you.

See the [Join](/join/) page for what I look for in PhD students, what working in
the group looks like, and how to reach out.
