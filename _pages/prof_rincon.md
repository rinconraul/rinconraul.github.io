#### [Raul Rincon](/bio/)

**Principal Investigator**

Raul Rincon is an incoming Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil,
Environmental, and Architectural Engineering at the University of Colorado
Boulder, joining in Fall 2026. He is interested in building computational methods
and tools that facilitate adaptive, resilient, and equitable performance of
structure-to-infrastructure systems. He envisions analyzing infrastructure
performance by considering its complex, multiscale, and evolving nature. He holds
a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from Rice University.
